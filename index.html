About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer currently working with early stage startups in the Bay Area.

My interest in tech and design started in the late 90s when I was only in middle school. I would spend my entire summer nerding out on HTML and Photoshop, creating websites for fun. Since then, I have found that UX is the perfect mix of design and technology and encompasses all that I am passionate about.
